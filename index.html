Undergraduate Economics Association @ MIT

Welcome to the website of the MIT Undergraduate Economics Association. The UEA is a group of undergraduates interested in economics who meet for the purpose of supporting academic endeavors, improving employment opportunities, and extending networks with each other and the outside world.

We'd love for you to join us at our talks, socials, and other events we host throughout the year!
